Sprinty — Character Mark Variations

Eight variations of the character mark concept. Each shown in light, dark, at two sizes, and in context on the home scene. The dark variants focus on warmth over eeriness.

1. Friendly Round
The original concept refined. Open eyes, warm smile, rosy cheeks. A small leaf accent. Friendly and approachable.

5. Line Art

6. Warm Glow
Specifically designed for dark mode. The face has a soft radiant glow — like a lamp in a dark room. The "someone left a light on for you" feeling. Dark eyes on a glowing face avoids the creepy inverted-eyes problem.

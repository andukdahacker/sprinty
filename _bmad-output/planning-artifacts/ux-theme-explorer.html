Coach Character Style Direction

How should the coach character look in the conversation view? These represent illustration style directions, not final art.

🎨
I. Illustrated / Flat
Like Headspace characters. Simple, warm, approachable. Clean lines, flat colors, friendly geometry. Maximum accessibility, minimum visual weight. Expressions through simple shape changes.
🖌️
II. Semi-Realistic / Painterly
Like a watercolor portrait. More gravitas, more emotional depth. Soft edges, organic textures, human-feeling warmth. Expressions carry more nuance. Higher art investment but stronger emotional connection.
✨
III. Iconic / Minimal
A simple, expressive silhouette or abstract form. Maximum abstraction — the coach is a presence, not a person. Expressions through gesture and glow, not facial features. Ethereal, universal, gender-neutral by default.
